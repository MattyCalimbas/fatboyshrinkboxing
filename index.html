My Journey

My name is Kenneth Han, but better known as Coach Fatboyshrink. 13 years ago, I stepped into my very 1st boxing gym at the age of 25 at a disgusting 320lbs. I was sick and tired of being fat. I wasn't chubby, overweight, or gained a few pounds ... I was legit FAT AF!

I've tried a lot of things to lose weight, but I knew Boxing was the answer after I took my very 1st class. Boxing helped me to drop an astonishing 132lbs and helped me to finally gain self-confidence as a man in every aspect of my life.

13 years later, I found my purpose in life by helping other men and women create their own Champion stories through the sport of boxing. Whether you want to transform your mind and body or simply want to learn high-level fundamental boxing, I am here to help you get there.
