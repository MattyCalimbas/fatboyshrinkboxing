Meet the Coach

13 Years of High Level Experience
10 Years of Teaching
132lbs Loss from 320lbs

My name is Kenneth Han, but better known as Coach Fatboyshrink.

13 years ago, I stepped into my very 1st boxing gym at the age of 25 at a disgusting 320lbs. I was sick and tired of being fat. I wasn't chubby, overweight, or gained a few pounds ... I was legit FAT AF!

Up until that point, I've lived in fear, self-doubt, and depression that manifested itself in out of control over eating. Bottom line, I was afraid that I would die young and alone. For once in my life, I wanted to taste something besides defeat!

I've tried a lot of things to lose weight, but I knew Boxing was the answer after I took my very 1st class. Boxing helped me to drop an astonishing 132lbs and helped me to finally gain self-confidence as a man in every aspect of my life.

13 years later, I found my purpose in life by helping other men and women create their own Champion stories through the sport of boxing. Whether you want to transform your mind and body or simply want to learn high-level fundamental boxing.

I am here to help you get there.
